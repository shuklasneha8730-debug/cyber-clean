Introduction

Cyber Clean is a campus-based awareness initiative designed to educate students about cyber hygiene practices. It focuses on safe password usage, protection from cyber threats, and responsible digital behavior.

Objectives of Cyber Clean

Raise Awareness

To spread knowledge about cyber hygiene practices.

Encourage Participation

To engage students in security workshops.

Password Safety

To educate on creating strong passwords.

Threat Prevention

To reduce cyber threats proactively.

Scope of Awareness

Target Audience

  • Faculty Members
  • Administrative Staff
  • IT Support Team
  • Campus Visitors

Key Topics
